# SourceStack — Scan Demo Content (batch-record kit)

**Goal:** 3 short vertical clips built around the Face Scan. Record all 3 in one sitting, post across 3–5 days. No daily reply grind. The tool is the content.

**Why this format:** the scan is a visual reveal (numbers populating + a score) — that's the dopamine. Zero-follower accounts pull views on reveal formats because the payoff is in the video itself, not your following.

## Setup (once, ~10 min)
- **Account:** looksmaxxing-native handle (e.g. `@sourcestack` or `@facescan.app`). Same handle TikTok + IG.
- **Bio:** one line + link. `Free face scan → 14 metrics, nothing uploads. Link below.` → link to `sourcestack.app/scan` (NOT the homepage — send them straight to the hook).
- **Link rule (learned from Reddit):** never put the URL in the caption or a comment — platforms suppress it. Link in bio only. Every video ends with "link in bio."
- **Recording:** iPhone screen record (Control Center → Screen Record) while you run the scan in the phone browser. Vertical, ~15–25s each. Film in good light so your own scan looks clean.

## The one metric that matters
Views are vanity. Watch **scan sessions** (analytics) in the 24h after each post. A clip that drives scans is a winner — make 3 more like it. A clip with views but no scans = bad CTA, fix the ending. That's your scoreboard.

## CLIP 1 — The Reveal (flagship)
*Relatability + payoff. This is the one most likely to travel.*

**Hook (film 3 versions, pick best in edit):**
- "I built a website that scores your face out of 10 using just a photo."
- "This free tool rates 14 things about your face in 30 seconds."
- "POV: you find the face scanner that actually tells you what to fix."

**Shot sequence:**
1. (0–2s) Hook text on screen over the scan landing page. Talk or text-only.
2. (2–6s) Tap to scan your own face → photo captures.
3. (6–14s) Metrics populate one by one — let them land. Overlay text: "jaw ✓ / canthal tilt / skin clarity…"
4. (14–18s) Final score reveals. React to it (even just a caption: "6.8 — not bad, here's what it said to fix").
5. (18–22s) Quick cut to the ranked "what to improve" list. End card: **"Try it free — link in bio."**

**On-screen text beats:** `14 metrics` → `nothing gets uploaded` → `your score ↓` → `+ a plan to fix it`
**Caption:** `Scanned my own face 😳 be honest what would you score`

## Batch plan
- **One session:** record all 3 (film 2–3 hook takes each). ~45 min.
- **Edit:** trim, add text overlays (CapCut). ~30 min each, or rough-cut and ship — done > perfect.
- **Post:** 1 clip every 1–2 days. Same clip can go TikTok + IG Reels + YouTube Shorts (3 platforms, 1 asset).
- **Repeat the winner:** whichever drives the most scan sessions, make 3 variations of that hook next batch.

## If nothing lands after ~6 posts
Not a signal the product is wrong — it's a hook problem. The scan visual is strong; iterate the first 2 seconds only. You made maps hit millions of views — same muscle: the hook is everything, the rest is delivery.
